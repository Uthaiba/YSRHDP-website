About YSRHDP

The Yobe State Rural Healthcare Development Program (YSRHDP) aims to improve healthcare access in rural communities across Yobe State. We focus on training healthcare workers, providing medical outreach, and supporting maternal and child health.

Our Mission: To enhance healthcare delivery in rural areas through innovation, education, and community support.

Our Vision: Healthy communities with equitable access to healthcare in Yobe State.
